About us

网络数据科学实验室旨在从网络的视角来观察现实世界中的各种复杂系统，并用真实的数据对这些系统进行建模，进而利用并发展网络科学和数据科学的理论、方法以及工具来更好地理解我们这个世界中各种复杂系统内部的联系并揭示它们的运行规律。实验室现有教师和学生共8人，其中副研究员1人，博士生2人，硕士生2人，本科生4人。实验室的主要研究方向为网络数据挖掘、社交网络分析、异常行为检测等，并在该领域发表论文20余篇。

Network data science lab focuses on the intersection researches of network science and data science. It aims to observe complex systems from the perspective of network, depict actual systems with real data, and use and develop the theories, methods and tools of network science and data science to better understand the internal relations of various complex systems in our world and reveal their operation laws. There are 8 people in the laboratory, including 1 associate researcher, 2 doctoral student, 1 master students and 4 undergraduate students. The main research directions are network data mining, social network analysis, anomaly detection, etc., and more than 20 papers have been published
